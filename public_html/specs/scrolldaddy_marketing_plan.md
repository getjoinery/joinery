# Specification: ScrollDaddy Marketing Plan

## Overview
This document outlines the strategic marketing plan for Scrolldaddy.app, focusing on cost-effective, high-impact growth strategies for a DNS-level content filtering and productivity tool.

## Product Value Proposition
*   **Core Message:** "Save your sanity online."
*   **Key Differentiator:** DNS-level blocking that bypasses app-level permissions and works across all devices with a 5-minute setup.
*   **Target Segments:** Productivity seekers (NoSurf), parents, privacy-conscious users, and individuals fighting digital addiction.

## Low-Hanging Fruit (The "Zero-User" Starting Path)
Designed for minimal effort and maximum automation via AI.

### 1. The "AlternativeTo" Blitz (30m Effort)
*   **Goal:** Passive high-intent traffic.
*   **Action:** Submit profile to [AlternativeTo.net](https://alternativeto.net).
*   **AI Assist:** "Write a 150-word product description for ScrollDaddy for AlternativeTo.net. Focus on 5-minute setup and DNS-level blocking as an alternative to NextDNS and Freedom.to."

### 2. The "Reddit Support Agent" Strategy (10m/Day)
*   **Goal:** Direct problem-solving.
*   **Action:** Search Reddit for "how to block social media on router" or "how to stop scrolling".
*   **AI Assist:** "A user is asking [PASTE POST HERE]. Write a helpful, non-salesy comment explaining why DNS-level blocking is effective and suggest ScrollDaddy as a simple tool."

### 3. Startup Directory Dump (1h Effort)
*   **Goal:** Fire-and-forget discovery.
*   **Action:** Submit to [BetaList](https://betalist.com/), [Indie Hackers](https://www.indiehackers.com/), and [10Words](https://10words.io/).
*   **AI Assist:** "Provide 3 taglines (60 chars) and 3 short descriptions (250 chars) focusing on 'Sanity' and 'Focus'."

## Ranked Marketing Strategies

### 1. Community Engagement (Reddit/Forums)
*   **Priority:** High | **Cost:** $0 | **Effort:** High
*   **Target Subreddits:** `r/NoSurf`, `r/Productivity`, `r/DigitalDetox`, `r/Parenting`, `r/SelfImprovement`.
*   **Tactics:** 
    *   Monitor "How do I block X?" threads.
    *   Provide value by explaining DNS-level vs. App-level blocking.
    *   Mention ScrollDaddy as a lightweight, privacy-focused solution.
*   **Success Metric:** Weekly referral traffic from Reddit.

### 2. AlternativeTo.net Listing & Optimization
*   **Priority:** High | **Cost:** $0 | **Effort:** Low
*   **Action:** Create/Claim ScrollDaddy profile.
*   **Competitors to target:** NextDNS, Freedom.to, Cold Turkey, AdGuard, OpenDNS.
*   **Tactics:** Request reviews from early adopters to boost ranking in the "Alternatives to NextDNS" list.

### 3. Product Hunt & Indie Hackers Launch
*   **Priority:** High | **Cost:** $0 | **Effort:** Medium
*   **Goal:** Social proof and a massive initial traffic spike.
*   **Assets Needed:** 
    *   "Why I built this" founder story.
    *   Clean dashboard screenshots.
    *   Exclusive "PH Community" discount code (e.g., `PH2026`).

### 4. Problem-Led SEO (Content Marketing)
*   **Priority:** Medium | **Cost:** Low | **Effort:** High
*   **Target Keywords:** 
    *   "How to block TikTok on router"
    *   "Best DNS for productivity"
    *   "Block social media on iPhone without Screen Time"
*   **Content Type:** Educational "How-to" guides that position the product as the easiest solution.

### 5. Comparison "War" Pages
*   **Priority:** Medium | **Cost:** $0 | **Effort:** Medium
*   **Goal:** Capture high-intent traffic comparing brands.
*   **Pages to Create:**
    *   *ScrollDaddy vs. NextDNS: Why focus matters.*
    *   *ScrollDaddy vs. Freedom.to: DNS-level vs. App-level.*

## Organic SEO Strategy (Passive Growth)
Focus on capturing users searching for specific problems related to digital addiction and productivity.

### 1. Keyword Focus (Targeting Intent)
*   **Problem-Solution Keywords:** "How to block TikTok on iPhone," "Stop doomscrolling," "Best DNS for parental controls."
*   **Comparison Keywords:** "ScrollDaddy vs NextDNS," "Best Freedom.to alternatives."
*   **Informational Keywords:** "Benefits of digital detox," "How DNS filtering works for productivity."

### 2. On-Page Optimization Checklist
*   **Title Tags:** Use the format `ScrollDaddy | [Feature/Problem] | Save Your Sanity Online`.
    *   *Example (Homepage):* `ScrollDaddy | Block Social Media & Distractions at the DNS Level`
*   **Meta Descriptions:** Highlight the "5-minute setup," "No traffic logging," and "Device-wide blocking" in every description.
*   **Header Tags (H1, H2, H3):** Ensure H1 contains the primary keyword (e.g., "DNS Filtering for Productivity").
*   **Image Alt Text:** Use descriptive text like "ScrollDaddy dashboard blocking Facebook" instead of generic filenames.

### 3. Comparison "War" Pages
Create dedicated landing pages for competitors to capture high-intent users looking to switch:
*   *NextDNS Alternative:* Highlight your focus on productivity features vs. their generic security focus.
*   *Freedom.to Alternative:* Highlight your network-level blocking vs. their app-level (easily bypassed) blocking.

### 4. Problem-Led Blog Content (AI-Generated)
Use AI to draft 500-word guides for:
*   "The Ultimate Guide to Blocking Social Media on Your Home Network."
*   "Why Browser Extensions Aren't Enough: The Case for DNS Filtering."
*   "Protecting Your Kids Without Invading Their Privacy."
