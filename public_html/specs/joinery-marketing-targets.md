# Joinery Marketing Targets (Exhaustive & Ranked)

This document identifies potential marketing targets for **Joinery**—a modular, PHP/PostgreSQL membership and event management platform. Targets are ranked by **Synergy Score** based on their alignment with Joinery's core identity: **Open Source, PHP/PostgreSQL, Modular Architecture, and Self-Hosted Membership/Events.**

---

## 🟢 Tier 1: Perfect Synergy (Score 9-10/10)
*These targets are the "Bullseye." Their audience values exactly what Joinery provides: clean PHP code, modularity, and ownership of data.*

### 1. Matthias Noback (Architecture Advocate)
*   **URL:** [matthiasnoback.nl](https://matthiasnoback.nl/)
*   **Synergy:** 10/10
*   **Why:** He is the leading voice on "Modular Monoliths" and decoupling from frameworks. Joinery’s architecture is a living example of his teachings.
*   **Approach:** Reach out for a code review or as a case study for his "Advanced Web Application Architecture" blog series.

### 2. Larry Garfield (PHP-FIG / Modular PHP)
*   **URL:** [crell.mit.edu](https://www.crell.mit.edu/)
*   **Synergy:** 10/10
*   **Why:** He focuses on high-quality PHP standards and functional patterns. He was a key figure in Drupal's modularity.
*   **Approach:** Highlight the PostgreSQL implementation and the "LogicResult" pattern used in Joinery.

### 3. Open Saas Directory / OpenAlternative
*   **URL:** [opensaasdirectory.com](https://opensaasdirectory.com) | [openalternative.co](https://openalternative.co)
*   **Synergy:** 10/10
*   **Why:** These platforms specifically promote open-source alternatives to proprietary SaaS (like WildApricot or Cvent).
*   **Approach:** Submit Joinery as the open-source alternative to WildApricot and Neon One.

### 4. Self-Hosted.show (Podcast)
*   **URL:** [selfhosted.show](https://selfhosted.show/)
*   **Synergy:** 9/10
*   **Why:** A dedicated audience of people who want to host their own software for business and personal use.
*   **Approach:** Pitch a segment on "Self-hosting your organization's membership" vs. using proprietary cloud tools.

### 5. Console (Developer Newsletter)
*   **URL:** [console.substack.com](https://console.substack.com/)
*   **Synergy:** 9/10
*   **Why:** They find and feature high-quality open-source tools for developers. They value unique stacks (like PHP/Postgres).
*   **Approach:** Submit a "Project Spotlight" featuring the modularity and the built-in deletion system.

## 🛠 Recommended Outreach Strategy

1.  **Technical Pitch (Tier 1):** Focus on the **modularity** and **PostgreSQL** integration. These are your peers. Ask for feedback or a technical interview.
2.  **Problem/Solution Pitch (Tier 2):** Focus on **Data Ownership** and **Cost Savings** compared to Cvent/WildApricot. Target IT Directors and Agency Owners.
3.  **Community Presence (Tier 3):** Focus on **Build in Public**. Share specific architectural decisions (e.g., "Why we didn't use an ORM") to spark discussion.
4.  **SEO & Discovery (Tier 4):** Simply ensure Joinery is **listed** and has **correct keywords** (e.g., "Open Source Membership Management").
